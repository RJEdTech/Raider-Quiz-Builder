AI prompt

Paste this into Claude, Flint, ChatGPT, or any chat AI. Then on the next line, tell it what kind of quiz you want — topic, number of questions, mix of types, anything else. The AI will return a file you can drop straight into the converter.

Example follow-up after pasting: "Generate a 15-question quiz on the French Revolution. Mix multiple choice, true/false, and one essay. Title it 'Unit 6 Check' with a 30-minute description."
